Fire and Safety

Fire Alarms

Both national and European laws demand a minimum level of fire alarm and protection equipment, and that it be maintained to accredited standards for all public and commercial buildings. Precision Electrical supplies, installs, maintains and even offer monthly testing and systems monitoring. We are NICEIC approved and Trust Mark registered so you can rest assured that we can not only handle any size of job but maintain the highest level of quality and workmanship possible. For a more tailored quote on electrical services based on fire risk assessments and especially for Fire Alarm systems in London and Greater London please contact us.



Emergency Lighting

Emergency lighting covers a broad range of equipment. The aim of emergency lighting is to ensure that exits and accesses are easily found. Emergency lights are a legal requirement in both new and existing commercial and high occupancy residential buildings. Emergency lights are battery-backed lighting devices which commence to operate automatically when a building experiences a power failure or in the event of fire. Precision Electrical will ensure that you meet all legal obligations and legislative requirements as well as help you find the right solution for your property.
